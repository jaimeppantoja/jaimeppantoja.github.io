Webscraping With Python

In this project, we web-scraped the Job Bank of Canada, particularly we got information about offers for Bookkeepers. The main idea of this script is to find the locations where the demand for this position is higher near Vancouver (distance 50km) and get some insights about the salary ranges.

With the information collected, we can see a pattern where 68% of the last 25 offers posted in Job Bank Canada were located in Surrey. This illustrates a high demand for bookkeepers in this location. Additionally, in terms of the salary range, most of the salaries are within the boundaries of 22 CAD and 28 CAD per hour.

The web-scrapping took into consideration the first 25 results and is sorted by the posted date. For this script, it was used libraries like BeautifulSoup, requests, matplotlib, pandas, and others. This example can be used as a guideline to look for similar information in different professions.
